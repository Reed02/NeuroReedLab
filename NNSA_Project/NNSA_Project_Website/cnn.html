Reed02 / NeuroReedLab
Phase 3 — Classification

CNN
& Model Training

Five scripts building from a binary baseline to an 11-class motor imagery classifier. The progression moves through raw time-series, spectrogram-based 2D CNNs, full multiclass EDF classification, and finally CNN training on precomputed FFT features, each step informed by what the previous one revealed.

PyTorch — Model Development
01
CNN Baseline
Binary Eyes Open / Eyes Closed classifier. Two-layer 1D CNN on raw 64-channel EEG. Establishes that the pipeline works before adding complexity.
› 02
Spectrogram CNN
Converts EDF to log-scaled 64×64 spectrograms. 2D CNN with BatchNorm, Dropout, early stopping, and LR scheduling. Four-class problem.
› 03
Multiclass EDF CNN
Full 11-class motor imagery classification on raw EDF time series. Stratified splits, per-class probability histograms, confusion matrix.
› 04
Multiclass FFT CNN
Classifies 4 motor conditions from precomputed average-FFT .mat feature vectors. Deeper 6-layer architecture with BatchNorm and Dropout.
› 05
Plot Avg FFT
Single-file sanity check: loads a .mat FFT feature vector and plots mean spectral amplitude 0–60 Hz. Validates MATLAB export before CNN training.
